About

I am currently a data scientist at Foursquare. I did my PhD in Computer Science from UC Riverside, where I was fortuante to be advised by Silas Richelson and Amey Bhangale. I am broadly interested in theoretical computer science. Particularly, my research mostly revolved around understanding different pseudorandom objects and their applications.
